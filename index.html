How to Report a Phishing Email

Throughout this tutorial you have been advised to report suspicious emails. Here is exactly how to do that at Northumbria University:

Option 1 - Report directly in Outlook

Select the suspicious email, click the three dots (...) in the top right corner, select Report then Report Phishing. This alerts Microsoft and the university IT team simultaneously.

Option 2 - Forward to IT Services

Forward the suspicious email to itservices@northumbria.ac.uk with the subject line Suspected Phishing Email. The IT team will investigate and block the sender if confirmed.

Option 3 - Contact IT directly

If you have already clicked a link or entered any details, contact the IT Service Desk immediately in person or by phone so they can secure your account straight away.

Remember: Never ignore a suspicious email. Reporting protects you and the entire university community.
